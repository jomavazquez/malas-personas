# Cookie Policy

**Last updated:** June 2026

## What are cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your device when you visit a website or use an application.

## Cookies we use

| Cookie | Type | Purpose |
|--------|------|---------|
| `token` | Session | Keep you logged in |
| `mp_language` | Preference | Remember your language preference |
| `guestId` | Session | Identify guest sessions |

We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

## How to disable them

You can delete or block cookies from your browser settings. Please note that disabling them may affect how the application works.
