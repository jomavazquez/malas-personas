Cookie Policy

Spoiler: I don't use cookies

I know. You came here expecting the usual wall of text about cookies, consent banners, privacy popups, and 47 advertising partners who desperately want to know what you had for breakfast last Tuesday.

Well, not here.

I don't use cookies.

Not to track you. Not to analyze every click you make. Not to build profiles. Not to sell data. Keeping the app running smoothly is already enough work.

So, what do I care about?

Performance.

Speed.

Letting you jump in, play, and leave without half the internet taking notes about you.

I'm not obsessed with metrics. I don't need to know how long you stared at a button or how many times you moved your mouse before hitting "Play".

I'd rather spend my time improving the experience than collecting analytics charts.

Alright, so how do I remember stuff?

This is where the silent hero steps in: localStorage. I use your browser's local storage to keep information that's necessary for the app to work properly and to make your experience better.

For example:

  • Keeping you signed in.
  • Remembering your preferences.
  • Saving app settings.
  • Preventing you from setting up the same things over and over again.

All of that information stays on your device and is only used to make the application work as intended.

Do I share that data?

No.

I don't sell data.

I don't rent it out.

I don't trade it for cookies either.

The quick version

🍪Cookies: nope.

👀Ad tracking: nope.

📈Invasive analytics: nope.

⚡Performance and user experience: absolutely.

💾localStorage to keep everything running nicely: yep.

And now that you know more about this cookie policy than most websites ever tell you, you can get back to what actually matters: playing.
