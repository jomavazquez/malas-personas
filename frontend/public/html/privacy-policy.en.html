Privacy Policy

1. Data Controller

The person responsible for processing personal data collected through this application is:

  • Owner: José María Vázquez
  • Application: Malas Personas (www.malaspersonas.com)
  • Contact: hola@malaspersonas.com

2. Data I Collect

Malas Personas can be played without creating an account. If you choose that option, no personal data is collected or stored.

If you decide to register, I only collect the information strictly necessary to manage your account:

  • Username: visible to other players during games.
  • Email address: used to identify your account and, in the future, for account-related communications if you request them.
  • Password: stored securely using hashing techniques (bcrypt). It is never stored in plain text and cannot be recovered by anyone, including the owner of the application.

I do not collect payment information, location data, or any personal information beyond what is listed above.

3. Why I Process Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Manage your user account and allow access to the application.
  • Display your username to other players during games.
  • Store the history of rooms you have created.

I do not use your data for advertising, commercial profiling, or share it with third parties under any circumstances.

4. Legal Basis

The processing of your data is based on the performance of the contractual relationship created when you register for the application (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR) and, where applicable, your explicit consent when accepting these terms.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Malas Personas does not use cookies of any kind. It also does not use tracking pixels, browser fingerprinting, or any other tracking mechanisms.

The application uses browser local storage (localStorage) only to store your language preference and, if you play as a guest, an anonymous session identifier generated on your device that is not linked to personally identifiable information.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained while your account remains active. If you request account deletion, all personal data will be removed within a maximum period of 30 days, unless I am legally required to retain certain information.

7. Your Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access: know what data I hold about you.
  • Rectification: correct inaccurate information.
  • Erasure: request deletion of your data.
  • Portability: receive your data in a structured format.
  • Objection: object to processing under certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, contact me at hola@malaspersonas.com. I will respond within a maximum of 15 days.

If you believe your data is not being processed appropriately, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Agency (AEPD) (www.aepd.es).

8. Security

I apply technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. These measures include:

  • Passwords are stored using bcrypt hashing and cannot be recovered.
  • Communication between your browser and the servers is protected using secure HTTPS encryption.
  • Database access is restricted and protected.

9. Changes to This Policy

I may update this policy at any time. When I do, I will update the date shown at the top of this document.
